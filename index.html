CONTRACT AGREEMENT BETWEEN DIRECT SELLING ENTITY AND DIRECT SELLER


The company or direct selling entity accepts the following terms and conditions:

  • The company will never charge you a penalty if you will terminate your participation as a direct seller.
  • You will never be asked to pay any minimum subscription or renewal charges.
  • You will never be forced to purchase any sales demonstration equipment or material or any other business advertising material.
  • The company will never force you to do the business of more than Rs 1200 in a particular year.
  • The company will be responsible for your grievances and assured you for its redressal.
  • The company will pay your incentives monthly with no delay.
  • I declare that I am 18 years old or more.
  • I am the resident of India.
  • I will never visit the prospect direct seller premises without prior appointment.
  • I will always carry my identity card for meeting with my prospect direct seller.
  • I will give complete explanations of business to the prospect direct seller like remuneration system, grievances redressal mechanism, business limit and all other details like name, address, website, legalities of the direct selling entity.
  • I will provide following information to the consumer at the time of sale, namely: (a) My name, address, contact number, distributor ID, identity proof and the details of direct selling entity; (b) A description of the goods or services to be supplied; (c) The goods return policy or services deactivating policy of the company in details before the transaction; (d) Time and place for inspection of the sample; (e) Details regarding the complaint redressal mechanism.
  • The company may terminate my participation as a direct seller if I will not do the business of at least Rs 1200 in a particular year.
  • I will not:- (a) Use misleading, deceptive and / or unfair trade practices; (b) Use misleading, false, deceptive, and / or unfair recruiting practices, including misrepresentation of actual or potential sales or earnings and advantages of direct selling to any prospective direct seller; (c) Make any factual representation to a prospective direct seller that cannot be verified or make any promise that cannot be fulfilled; (d) Present any advantages of direct selling to any prospective direct seller in a false and / or deceptive manner; (e) Make any representation relating to the direct selling operation, including remuneration system and agreement between the direct selling entity and the direct seller, or the goods and / or services being sold by such direct seller which is false and / or misleading; (f) Encourage or force direct sellers recruited by me to purchase goods and / or services in unreasonably large amounts; (g) Provide any literature and / or training material to a prospective and / or existing direct seller which has not been approved by the direct selling entity; (h) Force any prospective and / or existing direct seller to purchase any literature and / or training materials or sales demonstration equipment.
